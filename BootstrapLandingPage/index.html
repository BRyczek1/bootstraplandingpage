Catering

If you're hosting an event at WCTC, our in-house provider, Aladdin, can provide a variety of dining options for your event. This includes continental and hot breakfasts; reception trays; box lunches featuring traditional sandwiches, wraps or salad options; full lunch buffets; pizza; desserts; beverage service and much more. Alladin is the preferred provider for events held on campus.
